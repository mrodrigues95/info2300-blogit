Blog It - Your personal blogger and friend

Blog It is a personal blogging tool that will let you create, edit and post whatever you would like.

With Blog It you can:

- Create new blogs
- Edit recent blogs
- View all your blogs in the dashboard

You can start your blogging adventure by navigating to the "Create Blog" page and begin by creating your first blog.

Please feel free to contact me if you have any suggestions or features you would like to see implemented.
